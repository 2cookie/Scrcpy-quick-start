# Scrcpy-quick-start
Batch script to quickly connect Scrcpy to your device

# How to use
Step 1 download scrcpy from https://github.com/Genymobile/scrcpy

Once its unzipped place the batch file inn the same filder as scrcpy.exe

On your device go to developer options and enable USB debugging 

Connect your device to your pc via a usb cable and click trust on your device if this is not the first time connecting your device to this pc for usb debugging you wont need to do this again on most phones

Double click connect.bat and thats it! yay
